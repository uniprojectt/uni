Getting rid of addiction

  • Stay away from bad friends and addicts.
  • Increase awareness of the dangers of drugs.
  • Make sure to communicate with family and friends.
  • Learn healthy ways to deal with psychological stress.

  • Divide the day correctly to change the routine mood that leads to depression.
  • Sports, art, and meditation are ideal ways to protect young people from addiction.
  • Confront the problem that led you to addiction in a correct way to solve it without relying on anyone.
